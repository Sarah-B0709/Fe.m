I want to be a Front End Web Developer. Any tips for getting started in the field?

Response by Althea on January 30th, 2021

Here are a few things that helped me get started:

  1. Learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: These are the most imporatant web development languages, and you will need to be familiar with them in the field. Textbooks, online courses, and various instagram accounts or blogs can be helpful in learning.
  2. Seek out learning oppportunities wherever possible: The best way to learn more in the field is to get your hands dirty! Finding various learning opportunities whether through school or outside of it is critical for gaining confidence in your skills and improving.
  3. Be Patient: There is SO much to learn in the field of web development and you cannot expect to learn it all at once. Patience is critical for building your skills in the field

Hope this helps!
